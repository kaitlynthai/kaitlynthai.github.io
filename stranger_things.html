Main Characters:

Eleven:

Portrayed by Millie Bobby Brown, nicknamed "El", is a strange, young girl with psychokinetic abilities, an unusual appearance, and a limited vocabulary.

Michael Wheeler:

Portrayed by Finn Wolfhard, nicknamed "Mike", is the son of Karen and Ted, younger brother of Nancy and older brother to his younger sister Holly, and one of the three loyal friends of Will Byers.

Dustin Henderson:

Portrayed by Gaten Matarazzo, is one of Will's best friends with cleidocranial dysplasia, which he gets made bullied a lot for, in the first season.

Lucas Sinclair:

Portayed by Caleb Mclaughlin, is another of Will's best friends, older brother of Erica Sinclair and takes a while to warm up to eleven.

Will Byers:

Portayed by Noah Schnapp, vanishes early in the first season by encountering the "demogorgon" that takes him into the "Upside Down", a messed up copy of their world as an alternate dimension.

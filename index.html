All about Stranger Things

Stranger Things is an American, science and horror fiction based television series that was first released in the summer of 2016. As of right now, there has now been 3 seasons released in the past three years of production. Also, stranger things has become a very popular series to binge over audiences across the world and is one of netflix's originals.

Plot of the show:

When Will Byers suddenly goes missing, the whole town of Hawkins, Indiana, turns upside down. Many people are on the search for Will, including his mother, Joyce; his brother, Jonathan; his friends--Mike, Dustin, and Lucas; the police chief, Jim Hopper; and other notable people. But one thing leads to another, creating a supernatural trail. And things get even weirder when a little girl with a shaved head comes into the story.

Main Characters:

Eleven:

Portrayed by Millie Bobby Brown, nicknamed "El", is a strange, young girl with psychokinetic abilities, an unusual appearance, and a limited vocabulary.

Michael Wheeler:

Portrayed by Finn Wolfhard, nicknamed "Mike", is the son of Karen and Ted, younger brother of Nancy and older brother to his younger sister Holly, and one of the three loyal friends of Will Byers.

Dustin Henderson:

Portrayed by Gaten Matarazzo, is one of Will's best friends with cleidocranial dysplasia, which he gets made bullied a lot for, in the first season.

Lucas Sinclair:

Portayed by Caleb Mclaughlin, is another of Will's best friends, older brother of Erica Sinclair and takes a while to warm up to eleven.

Will Byers:

Portayed by Noah Schnapp, vanishes early in the first season by encountering the "demogorgon" that takes him into the "Upside Down", a messed up copy of their world as an alternate dimension.
